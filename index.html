Deposits are fast, while withdrawals enforce a seven-day challenge period to protect against fraudulent state transitions.

  • Fraud-proof window — Approximately seven days allow challenges to invalid withdrawals, strengthening Ethereum-level security.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to bridge Optimism?

Deposits to bridge Optimism usually take 2–10 minutes after Ethereum confirmation. Withdrawals back to mainnet require about seven days due to the fraud-proof challenge period.

How much does it cost to bridge Optimism?

Costs depend on Ethereum gas fees at the time of deposit. Expect anywhere from a few dollars to over $30 during congestion, plus minimal Layer 2 execution fees.

Is bridge Optimism safe for large amounts?

Bridge Optimism is generally secure through its non-custodial design, but smart contract risk always exists. For large amounts, test with a small transaction first.

What if my bridge Optimism transaction is stuck?

First, check Ethereum transaction status on a block explorer. If pending, you may need to speed up or replace the transaction in your wallet.

Can I cancel a bridge Optimism transaction?

You can only cancel before confirmation by replacing the pending Ethereum transaction with a higher-gas zero-value transfer. Once confirmed, deposits cannot be reversed.

Do I need ETH to bridge Optimism?

Yes, ETH on Ethereum mainnet is required to pay gas for the deposit transaction. Without ETH, the bridge transaction cannot execute.

Can I bridge Optimism tokens other than ETH?

Yes, many ERC-20 tokens like USDC are supported. Always verify token compatibility and official contract addresses before confirming.

Is using an exchange better than bridge Optimism?

Direct exchange withdrawals to Optimism can be faster and sometimes cheaper. However, they rely on custodial infrastructure instead of direct smart contract interaction.
