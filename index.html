What is Bridge Optimism?

OP Mainnet Gateway is the Optimism Foundation-linked bridge interface for moving assets between Ethereum and OP Mainnet. The underlying Standard Bridge supports ETH and most standard ERC-20 tokens.

How Bridge Optimism works

  1. Open the gatewayUse the official app URL and connect a wallet you control.
  2. Choose directionSelect Ethereum to OP Mainnet for deposits, or OP Mainnet to Ethereum for withdrawals.
  3. Pick asset and amountReview token, network, recipient, and wallet approval details before signing.
  4. Track settlementDeposits are typically faster; withdrawals to Ethereum include the protocol challenge period.

Before you bridge

Built for OP Mainnet

The app routes users to Optimism's official bridge surface rather than a third-party landing flow.

Clear L1 to L2 path

OP Mainnet is an Ethereum Layer 2; official docs describe bridging as moving data and tokens between the two networks.

Verify before signing

Confirm token addresses, chain IDs, and the app domain; OP Mainnet details are listed in Optimism network information.

Bridge Optimism FAQ

What is bridge optimism used for?

It is used to route users to the official Optimism bridge app for moving ETH or supported ERC-20 tokens between Ethereum and OP Mainnet.

Is bridge optimism the official Optimism app?

This page is not the app; the official action happens at app.optimism.io/bridge. Always verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

How long does bridge optimism take?

Optimism docs state Ethereum to OP Mainnet transfers through the Standard Bridge are usually completed within 1-3 minutes, while OP Mainnet to Ethereum withdrawals complete in 7 days because of the challenge period.

Which tokens can I bridge optimism with?

The Standard Bridge supports ETH and most standard ERC-20 tokens, but it does not support fee-on-transfer or rebasing tokens according to Optimism documentation.

Do I need ETH to bridge optimism?

Yes, expect to need ETH for gas on the sending network, and review wallet prompts carefully before approving or signing any transaction.
